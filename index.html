Tribute Page for Katherine Johnson

Katherine Johnson was a mathemathician whose work on orbital engineering at NASA was crtitical for the success of the intial crewed US space missions.

  1. 26 Aug 1918
    Born Creola Katherine Coleman on August 26, 1918, in White Sulphur Springs, West Virginia.
  2. 1928
    Enrolled in the high school on the campus of West Virginia State College
  3. 1933

    Graduated high school and enrolled at West Virginia State

  4. 1937

    Graduated summa cum laude in 1937, with degrees in mathematics and French.

    Took a teaching job at a black public school in Marion, Virginia

  5. 1939

    After marrying her first husband, she left her teaching job and enrolled in a graduate maths program.
